Highlights — March 2026

Relevant Operations targeted by MLRO

New operations and investigations processed in March 2026, sourced from the MLRO Jira Board.

🔴

Operação Compliance Zero — Banco Master / BRB

In Progress

Federal Police investigation (originally launched Nov/2025) into a billion-dollar fraud scheme involving Banco Master and Banco de Brasília (BRB). Document received from Legal team with list of individuals to evaluate.

MLRO is evaluating the PDF for additional accounts, researching people and companies cited in media, and assessing related entities.

MLRO-206Source: Legal
🔴

Operação Núcleo Político CV no Amazonas

Done

Investigation into a scheme structured by the Comando Vermelho (CV) in collaboration with public officials, operating to bring drugs from Colombia and distribute from Amazonas. 8 individuals arrested including court officials, legislative staff, and police officers.

High cancellation rate of clients without transactions. Two with judicial blocks were reported to COAF.

Clear: 2Reported & Canceled: 2Canceled: 3No account: 1
🟠

Levantamento INSS × Agibank

Done

Investigation into INSS suspension of Agibank consigned credit operations due to serious irregularities. Nubank clients receiving values from Agibank accounts were analyzed (credits, debits, account status, last report).

Conclusion (23/Mar): Inconclusive study — demand finalized. Suspicious cases will be flagged through existing Prev. Fraudes and PLD/FTP rules.

MLRO-154Inconclusive
📊

Casos Reportados com Período Divergente

Done

Due to an Analytics issue, ~1,000 alerts were generated and reported with wrong period (Dec+ instead of Nov+). ABR analysis using Glean compared summaries:

• 1,191 total cases • 269 with volume >2x • 226 with new risk counterparty • 7 with risk counterparty ≥20% • 2 cases requiring rectification to COAF

ABR approved by Fabricio. Remaining cases receive mitigation note in Holmes timeline.

The MLRO is part of the Financial Crime Risks structure and operates under the delegation of the Responsible Director for AML/CTF as outlined in Circular Letter 3,978 and CVM Resolution 50.

MLRO Report is a monthly document that consolidates the main topics, indicators, and highlights of AML/CFT activities, aimed at keeping the Board informed about the most relevant matters.
