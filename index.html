Meeting held at SPA's request to present key changes to Ordinance 566. IFS received the draft and may submit suggestions — deadline 17/03. Revisions to document and MSAC items completed.

MCA Rule — New Exploratory Alerts

Rule developed based on sample analysis of high-risk behaviours (high outbound flows to Global Account, merchant concentration, frequent in-and-out flows). Two new alerts proposed:

MCA High AmountCustomers transacting above threshold with volumes above income + recurring behavior
MCA Round TrippingCustomers redeeming ≥70% of funds above threshold + recurring behavior
